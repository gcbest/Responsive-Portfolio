About Me

Call me Godfrey. I am the product of two variables who originated from the South Caribbean Sea but followed the trail of opportunity to end up in the foreign land of Newark, New Jersey. I'm currently enrolled in the Rutgers Coding Bootcamp in order to continue along that trail and learn some new valuable skills along the way.

I'm deeply fascinated by how techonology enables us to store and transmit vast amounts of information in fractions of a second. I hope to be able to understand how this works and add something meaningful to this process. I enjoy most activites which are competitive and repetitive, which is what drew me to this coding bootcamp. The feeling of iteratively improving at a certain task is something that motivates me in any challenge I undertake.
